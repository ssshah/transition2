Blockchain development

Our blockchain development services span the delivery of secure decentralized solutions for various domains, and integration of blockchain-based modules into existing software to ensure data integrity and compliance, and reduce fraud.

ICO development

We offer blockchain consulting and technology support to launch ICO campaigns, including token design, cryptocurrency wallet development, smart contract implementation, landing page and admin part creation, and infrastructure maintenance.

Cryptocurrency development

We build cryptocurrencies on top of existing platforms, and develop decentralized cryptocurrency platforms from scratch equipping them with wallets for secure transfers, payments and exchanges.

Smart contracts development & audit

From simple transactions within a marketplace to supply chain tracking, we implement smart contracts across all blockchain-enabled deals and agreements.

We create smart contracts to support crowdfunding or automate business operations and commercial transactions using Ethereum or other platforms.

Related project

Blockchain
trading desk

Our team was tasked with blockchain application development. The customer required the delivery of a trading desk tool that would integrate the latest information from three major Bitcoin exchanges: Bitfinex, Bitstamp, and Kraken.

The delivered solution allows trading right from the tool based on the best exchange rates available. Similarly to Forex, the process can be configured to be semi-automatic, where the platform acts as an 'agent' that helps to place bids and offers based on the criteria submitted.

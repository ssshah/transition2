QA & Testing

Independent QA

We deliver independent QA services for any third-party software solution to give you better control of the application quality, help evaluate the product’s compliance with the original requirements and give you valuable insights into end user impressions of the product.

Integrated Testing

Any software development project at Itransition has an integral testing part within. Before we release your software, it goes through a thorough testing process to ensure it is working in the intended manner thus ensuring deployment in the most efficient way on your side.

QA Consulting

Documented analysis of your project along with fact- and experience-oriented recommendations for either proactive process improvement or simply responding to challenges. Implementation of best-practice risk-based and business-driven test strategies over the lifecycle of your software project portfolio.

Software Testing

Full-Cycle QA

From requirement analysis through development, implementation and maintenance of solutions, we apply the right type of testing, either manual or automated, matching each particular stage of your software product lifecycle.

Custom Testing

From web, desktop, mobile applications to server-side systems and devices, we create a comprehensive testing mix for each application to ensure consistent quality. We test both end-to-end solutions and their discrete elements/aspects.

  • Functional testing
  • Security testing
  • Compatibility testing
  • Performance testing
  • Usability testing
  • Localization testing

Documentation

From context-sensitive help for mobile, web or desktop apps to video user manuals, we deliver different types and formats of clearly structured documents compliant with your style guidelines for various target audiences.

Proofread by domain experts/native speakers, translated into 15+ languages, and readability tested (e.g. Flesch-Kincaid and Gunning fog index for documentation in English, etc).

Pre-Certification & Compliance Testing

With more than a decade of experience, we have tested hundreds of products for compliance before certification, and we can help you identify and fix possible issues thus making obtaining certifications as quick and easy as possible.

Managed Testing

Itransition offers a fully customized Managed Testing service where we take the responsibility of applications or systems testing at an enterprise or program level.

We provide a shared model to companies that want to transform their testing function to achieve measurable quality improvements without overheads and day-to-day responsibilities, while keeping overall control with KPIs aligned with business goals. We supply a core testing team with deep technological know-how and domain-specific expertise to embed our capabilities into your organization and ensure consistent quality across multi-supplier software projects.
